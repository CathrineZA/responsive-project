Why Johanneburg?

Johannesburg, the city of gold

Johannesburg is also known by its Zulu name, eGoli, meaning “city of gold,” because it was founded after gold was discovered on the site in 1886. The city once provided more than 40 percent of the world's annual gold production

Johannesburg's early history is the story of gold. In 1853 Pieter Jacob Marais, a South African prospector, recovered alluvial gold from the Jukskei River, north of what would become Johannesburg.

My favorite restuarants in Johannesburg

Grill house

The Grillhouse Sandton is a regular mealtime destination for local and international business-people, as well as being a firm family favourite restaurant. And while The Grillhouse is famous for its steaks and ribs, the menu caters for everyone, with a range of salads, chicken, seafood and vegetarian dishes on offer (not to mention the legendary brownies and ice cream).

Address:

Building 1, 11 Alice Ln, Sandhurst, Sandton, 2196

What I like about it

They are also extremely passionate about the alcohol on offer, boasting one of the largest choices of spirits, and have an extensive wine list as well as a well-stocked wine cellar with unique labels.

Taste kitchen

Taste Kitchen is a restaurant specialising in bespoke events, giving clients an African culinary experience with a modern twist. Taste Kitchen opened its doors July 2018, and has since been hosting intimate bespoke events showcasing Chef Nti's modern take on traditional African meals. The concept is simple: guests gather around a table for a minimum of three hours whilst they get served cubes of some of the finest cuisine on offer.

Address:

296A Fox St, Maboneng, Johannesburg, 2094

What I like about it

The restaurant offers an exclusive African experience through food, music, ar, and tourism. The restaurant caters for brunch, dinner, and breakfast
